Padded Camp

July 10-13, 2017
Grades 3-8


The purpose of this fully padded, full contact camp is to teach tackle football skills and drills used by the high school program, and to emphasize safe and fun techniques.

We will focus on offensive and defensive instruction and skill development. Individual instruction will be complimented with team drills. Proper blocking and tackling skills will be highlighted each day.

Mountain Vista High School players and staff, including Head Coach Ric Cash, will provide coaching and instruction. Low camper to coach ratios to ensure individual attention

The fee for the camp is $125 – Each camper will receive a Nike Eagle Elite camp shirt. This camp will fill up fast! Register early to guarantee a spot and ensure the correct size of shirt. Registration deadline is June 20th .
**Teams with 8 or more players will receive discounted rate of $110/player**

Non-Padded Camp

July 10-13, 2017
Grades 3-8


The purpose of this non-padded camp is to teach skills and drills used by the high school program, and to reinforce the fact that football is a game and should be fun.

We will focus on offensive and defensive instruction and skill development. Individual instruction will be complimented with team drills. Speed and agility testing similar to that of the NFL Combine will also take place.

Mountain Vista High School players and staff, including Head Coach Ric Cash, will provide coaching and instruction. *Low camper to coach ratios to ensure individual attention*

The fee for the camp is $85 – Each camper will receive a Nike Eagle Elite camp shirt. This camp will fill up fast! Register early to guarantee a spot and ensure the correct size of shirt. Registration deadline is June 20th.
